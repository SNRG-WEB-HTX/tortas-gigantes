2018 - Hoy

Nuestra Historia(Our Story)

El Comienzo(The Beginning)

All stories start somewhere but mine and my family's started in 2018. Our original name was Delicias y Mas Genesis, an ice cream shop filled with dreams but empty of customers.

(Todo comenzó en 2018. Nuestro nombre original era Delicias y Mas Genesis, una heladería llena de sueños pero vacía de clientes.)

La Lucha(The Struggle)

We made no sales the first year. The struggle was real, but we kept pushing forward with faith and determination.

(No hicimos ventas el primer año. La lucha era real, pero seguimos adelante con fe y determinación.)

COVID-19: El Punto Más Oscuro(The Darkest Point)

Then COVID-19 struck. The pandemic made staying open nearly impossible. We sold our cars, personal belongings, made tamales and barbacoa—anything to survive. Many times we'd arrive to find the lights cut off because we couldn't pay the bills.

(Llegó COVID-19. La pandemia hizo casi imposible mantenernos abiertos. Vendimos nuestros carros, cosas personales, hicimos tamales y barbacoa—cualquier cosa para sobrevivir.)

La Chispa de Esperanza(The Spark of Hope)

Right when we were about to give up, we looked at the luchador masks my father had sent from Mexico. They gave us a spark of hope—a call for change. Growing up with lucha libre in Mexico City, I knew this was our destiny.

(Justo cuando íbamos a rendirnos, miramos las máscaras de luchador que mi padre había enviado de México. Nos dieron una chispa de esperanza—un llamado al cambio.)

El Renacimiento(The Rebirth)

We became Tortas Gigantes el Enmascarado. We added giant tortas to our menu—the only place in Houston selling them. Houstonians were amazed by the size. Food influencers came, made videos that went viral, and reached thousands.

(Nos convertimos en Tortas Gigantes el Enmascarado. Agregamos tortas gigantes a nuestro menú—el único lugar en Houston vendiéndolas.)

Houston's Famous Giant Tortas(Today)

Each day we're blessed with long lines of customers waiting for "Houston's famous giant tortas." Our family's hard work, our choice to never give up, and our trust in God brought us here.

(Cada día somos bendecidos con largas filas de clientes esperando por las "famosas tortas gigantes de Houston.")

Tortas Gigantes el Enmascarado is now officially a franchise, working hard to give the best service and quality that identifies us.
